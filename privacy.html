Privacy Policy

Essential Audio QBO Connector · Last updated June 17, 2026

This connector is private, internal software used only by Essential Audio Inc. to read its own QuickBooks Online accounting data. There are no other users. This policy explains what it touches and what happens to that data.

What data it accesses

The connector reads accounting data from Essential Audio's own QuickBooks Online company — the kind of records covered by Intuit's accounting scope (invoices, payments, transactions, accounts, and similar). It reads this data only. It does not create, change, or delete anything in QuickBooks.

How that data is handled

  • The connector runs locally on Essential Audio's own computers. It is not a hosted web service.
  • Data moves only between the company's own machine and Intuit's QuickBooks API. It is not sent anywhere else.
  • The OAuth credentials used to connect are stored locally in an access-restricted file on the company's own machine.
  • No accounting data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party.
  • There is no analytics, no tracking, and no cookies.

How access is revoked

Access can be revoked at any time from inside QuickBooks Online: go to Settings → Manage apps (Connected apps), find this connector, and choose Disconnect. Access can also be revoked through the Intuit account. Once disconnected, the connector can no longer read any data.
