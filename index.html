Essential Audio QBO Connector

A private, read-and-write connector between Essential Audio Inc.'s own QuickBooks Online company and its own internal AI assistant.

This is internal software built and used by Essential Audio Inc., a commercial audio-video integration company. It is not a product, it is not sold, and it is not available to anyone outside the company.

What it does

The connector runs locally on Essential Audio's own computers. It reads and writes accounting data in the company's own QuickBooks Online company file so an internal AI assistant can automate its bookkeeping and reconciliation. It uses the com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting (read and write) scope, so it can create and update accounting records — entries, reconciliations, and similar — in addition to reading them.

  • Single company, single user: Essential Audio Inc. only.
  • Read and write access to the company's own accounting data, for internal bookkeeping and reconciliation.
  • Runs locally. No hosted service, no third-party servers.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no cookies.
