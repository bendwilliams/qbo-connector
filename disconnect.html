Disconnect the Connector

The Essential Audio QBO Connector can be disconnected at any time. Once it's disconnected, it can no longer read or write any data in QuickBooks Online.

How to disconnect

From inside QuickBooks Online:

  1. Sign in to QuickBooks Online.
  2. Go to Settings (the gear icon).
  3. Open Manage apps (also shown as Connected apps).
  4. Find Essential Audio QBO Connector in the list.
  5. Choose Disconnect.

Access can also be revoked through the Intuit account that authorized the connection. After disconnecting, the stored OAuth credentials on the local machine are no longer valid and can be removed.
